About Tech Fusion

Tech Fusion is a professionally managed electrical control panel manufacturing company based in Delhi, India, built on a strong foundation of engineering excellence and practical industry experience. With over 30+ years of combined expertise, we specialize in delivering reliable and standards-compliant electrical control panel solutions.

We operate as part of a well-established group of electrical solution providers and work in close association with our sister concern, Advanced Electricals. This long-standing association strengthens our technical capabilities, operational reliability, and understanding of diverse industrial requirements.

Our experience spans across multiple industries, enabling us to design and manufacture electrical control panels that meet real-world operational challenges. We focus on precision engineering, quality workmanship, and strict adherence to safety and performance standards.

Tech Fusion manufactures a comprehensive range of panels including LT Panels, MCC Panels, PCC Panels, PLC Automation Panels, APFC Panels, VFD Panels, and Distribution Panels, customized to suit industrial and commercial applications.

Backed by a skilled technical team and robust quality processes, we continue to deliver dependable, efficient, and future-ready electrical control panel solutions that customers can trust.
